# Key Insights from Product Behavior Analysis

## Traffic Acquisition
Google search drives the highest share of traffic, indicating strong organic visibility for the store.

## Product Discovery
Only about 23% of users who land on the website proceed to view a product page. This suggests there is an opportunity to improve product discovery on the homepage.

## Promotion Performance
Promotions have a click-through rate of roughly 14%. This indicates that users are seeing promotions but not engaging with them frequently.

## Device Usage
Desktop users represent around 67% of the traffic, while mobile users account for roughly 33%. Improving the mobile browsing experience could increase engagement.
